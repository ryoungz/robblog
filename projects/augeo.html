Web redesign to move the Augeo Marketing corporate site to a more interactive and multimedia centered experience.

My Role

Sole front-end developer on the Augeo Marketing redesign, working alongside a small team of talented Augeo designers. I was integral to the development process throughout the project and was lead developer for internal development.

Technologies

The build is a custom ExpressionEngine (EE) design that incorporates CSS animations and video elements. EE was used to provide freedom for multiple users to update content while also giving developers the power and flexibility to scale as needed.
