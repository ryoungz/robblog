Bio

Hi I'm Robert. I'm an Interactive Developer for Augeo Marketing in St. Paul, Minnesota. There I focus on building beautifully usable web experiences.

I've been coding ever since I received my first computer on my 7th Christmas. I have a passion for development that pushes me to constantly learn new technologies and techniques to expand my skill set. I love what I do and in the end I believe my work displays that.

When I am not immersed in a block of code or thumbnailing ideas for a new project, you will find me salsa dancing, digging in record store bins for vinyl, and geeking over the latest movies and television series.

Specialties: Front-end development, CSS(Sass), HTML, JavaScript, PHP, ExpressionEngine, ReactJS, and D3.js.
